Eco-Friendly Green Cleaning Trends

Sustainable floor cleaning practices for modern facilities

Sustainability is no longer optional for facility managers. Green cleaning practices reduce environmental impact, improve indoor air quality, and can lower operating costs.

Key Green Cleaning Trends

1. Chemical-Free Cleaning (ec-H2O)

Electrically activated water (ec-H2O) technology uses tap water and electricity to create a cleaning solution that removes dirt without chemicals. Benefits include:

— 70%+ reduction in chemical usage
— Lower chemical costs
— Reduced training requirements for safe chemical handling
— Better sustainability reporting

2. Low-Moisture Cleaning

Modern scrubbers use less water while maintaining cleaning effectiveness. Low-moisture systems mean:

— Faster drying times (floors ready in minutes)
— Less water consumption
— Reduced slip-and-fall risk
— Lower wastewater disposal costs

4. HEPA Filtration

High-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filters capture 99.97% of airborne particles. For facilities with air quality requirements, HEPA-filtered scrubbers improve indoor air quality during cleaning.

Making the Switch

Transitioning to green cleaning practices doesn't have to happen overnight. Start with one facility or one shift, measure the savings in chemical costs and water usage, and expand from there.
