About BIOCCE

From a small workshop in Shanghai to a global cleaning equipment manufacturer — our journey is driven by quality, precision, and partnership.

Our Story

Founded in Shanghai, China, BIOCCE Supply Chain Co., Ltd. has grown into a trusted manufacturer of professional cleaning equipment. With over 30 years of collective industry experience, we design and build machines that deliver consistent performance in demanding environments.

Our 15,000 m² manufacturing facility houses advanced production lines, rigorous quality control systems, and a dedicated R&D team that continuously refines our product designs. Every machine that leaves our factory undergoes strict inspection to ensure it meets international standards.

Today, BIOCCE partners with distributors, dealers, and OEM clients in over 50 countries worldwide. We remain committed to delivering reliable cleaning solutions at factory-direct prices.
