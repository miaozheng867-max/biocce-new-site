Floor Scrubbers for Manufacturing Plants

Choosing the right industrial floor scrubber for your factory floor

Manufacturing plants present some of the toughest floor cleaning challenges. Oil, grease, metal shavings, dust, and heavy foot traffic create conditions that require robust equipment.

Key Considerations for Factory Floors

Floor Surface Type

Most manufacturing plants have sealed concrete, unsealed concrete, or epoxy-coated floors. Each requires a different approach:

Floor TypeRecommended BrushNotes
Sealed concreteMedium nylon brushGentle enough for the sealant, effective on dirt
Unsealed concreteStiff polypropylene brushHandles rough surfaces and embedded grime
Epoxy-coatedSoft pad driverAvoid aggressive brushes that damage coating

Debris Type

Manufacturing plants accumulate various debris types. A scrubber with a pre-sweep option or debris tray can handle metal shavings, bolts, and packaging materials before they damage the squeegee.

Recommended Machine Specs

FeatureRecommended
Machine typeRide-on for plants over 30,000 sq ft
BatteryLithium-ion for multi-shift operations
Scrub path28-36 inches for efficient coverage
Tank capacity50+ gallons solution tank
Water typeHot water capable for grease removal

Pro tip: For plants with heavy oil buildup, use a pre-spray degreaser and allow dwell time before scrubbing. This prevents oil from spreading across the floor.
