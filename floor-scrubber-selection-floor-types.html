Floor Scrubber Selection by Floor Type

Tile, epoxy, concrete, and vinyl — choose the right machine for your surface

Choosing the wrong brush or pad for your floor type can cause permanent damage. This guide helps you select the right floor scrubber setup for your specific floor surface.

Brush vs Pad: Which to Use

Cylindrical brushes — Best for rough concrete and grooved surfaces. They agitate more deeply and pick up debris better.

Disc pads — Best for smooth surfaces like tile, epoxy, and polished concrete. They provide consistent pressure and a streak-free finish.

Common mistake: Using a stiff brush on polished concrete or marble. One pass can etch the surface permanently. Always test a small area first.
