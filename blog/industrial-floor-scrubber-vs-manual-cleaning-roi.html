Payback Period Calculation

Additional Cost Savings

Beyond labor, floor scrubbers deliver savings in several other areas:

  • Water consumption: Scrubbers use 70-80% less water than mops
  • Chemical usage: Precise dosing reduces detergent costs by 50%
  • Floor longevity: Proper machine cleaning extends floor life by 3-5 years
  • Safety: Reduced slip-and-fall incidents lowers insurance and liability costs
  • Productivity: Clean environment improves worker efficiency by 5-10%

Real Case: 15,000 m² Fulfillment Center

A fulfillment center in Europe was spending 8 hours per shift on manual floor cleaning (2 workers, 16 hours total daily). After deploying a single BC1000 ride-on scrubber:

  • Cleaning time reduced to 2 hours per shift
  • Cleaning labor budget reduced from $43,000/year to $7,500/year
  • Machine cost: $5,800, paid back in 2 months
  • Cleaner floors led to 15% reduction in inventory errors
